RBM Real Estate Services

Your real estate needs aren't like anyone else's.
Why should your broker be like everyone else?

Bart McLeroy is a real estate consultant with over 20 years of real estate experience that includes property management, multi-property portfolio management, commercial sales and leasing, international real estate transactions, and strategic consulting. Whatever your real estate needs are, he can put his experience and expertise to work for you.

Bart is a licensed Texas Real Estate Broker and registered Property Tax Consultant. He is also a licensed Washington Managing Real Estate Broker. He is a member of the North Texas Commercial Association of Realtors, the Collin County Association of Realtors, and the National Association of Real Estate Advisors. He holds the Accredited Consultant in Real Estate (ACRE) designation, and has earned advanced degrees in real estate and logistics consulting. 

What is a real estate consultant? Most real estate agents work solely on commission, meaning that if you aren't buying, selling, or leasing, they can't help you. A real estate consultant is equipped to advise you on a wide range of real estate-related issues, even if you're not ready to buy, sell or lease at this time.

 Property Tax Advisory

When was the last time you protested your property's tax assessment? We have extensive experience with the tax protest process, and can offer advice on how to proceed.

Relocation or Retirement

Are you moving, either for retirement or a new job? Many homeowners assume their best option is to sell their current house and buy in their new community, but the answer is not always so straightforward. Let us provide you with data to help you make your decision.

Looking to Purchase

Are you looking for a new home to purchase? Start your search with mls.rbmrealestate.com and then let us know when you're ready to move on to the next step!

Looking to Rent

Shopping for the best apartment deals requires having the most up-to-date information possible. Start your search with apartments.rbmrealestate.com and then let us know when you're ready to get a complete report on your preferred communities!

Business Start-Ups

Are you starting up a business and need advice regarding your current and future real estate needs? Planning at the early stages of your new business can save you money and problems in the future.

Purchasing an Existing Business

Are you considering purchasing an existing business that either owns or leases their real estate? Understanding your options and obligations are essential to minimizing your costs and maximizing your new business's revenue. (We can help with that!) 

Sole Occupant

Are you a tenant who occupies 100% of your building? Do you need guidance about renewing your current lease, relocating, or purchasing your building? Let us help!

Business Expansion

Are you currently leasing space but your business is growing? We can help you establish a real estate strategy that will support your growth without weighing you down.
