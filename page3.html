Property Tax Consulting

You need to protest your property taxes every year. We can help.

Hi, my name is Bart McLeroy. I'm a property tax consultant and a real estate broker. Click here if you want to get more information about me.

No one likes having to protest their property taxes every year. It takes time and effort to gather your data, and then you have to navigate a system that often seems confusing. Below I am going to tell you why you should be protesting your property taxes every year, and how we can help.

By not protesting your property value, you are paying more property taxes than you should. It's really that simple. Imagine a scenario where all of your neighbors protest their property values and you don't. Whatever the outcome of their protest is, over time they are going to be reducing their property's taxable value, and their property taxes, while you will be paying more than you should.

I like to think of this as preventative maintenance. It's like having an air conditioning tuneup every year, or changing the oil on your car, or getting insurance quotes every so often. Consider me your property tax mechanic.

Negotiated Settlement

Many times, a fair value can be negotiated with the appraisal district without the need to go to a formal hearing. This requires knowing your property's value and condition, and also knowing how the appraisal district works. Rest assured we will work to get you the best deal we can.

Appraisal Review Board Hearing

Sometimes a fair settlement can't be reached informally, and evidence must be submitted at a formal hearing before the Appraisal Review Board (ARB). This can be a stressful and time-consuming process. Not to worry, though - we've got you covered! Your representation package includes ARB representation as well.

Fixed Fees

Most property tax consultants charge a fee based on the amount that your taxable value is reduced, even if it doesn't actually save money on your taxes. That means that your fee could be $0 one year and thousands of dollars the next. That's hard to budget for! With our service, you always know exactly what you'll be paying every year.

Easy to Budget For

I don't know about you, but I pay for a lot of subscription services. I pay $20 / month for Netflix, and I rarely use it. I pay $46 / month to get unlimited car washes for me and my wife. Now, I like a clean car, but I also know that the low cost of our property tax representation package will provide more lasting financial results than a streaming service.

Annual Check-In

I will check in at the beginning of every year to get updated information about your property's condition. This is because the value of a property for a tax year is set on January 1. So if you have damage or needed repairs, documenting that increases the odds of us getting a favorable result in your protest.
